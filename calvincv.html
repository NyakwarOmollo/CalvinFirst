About Me

Dr. Calvin Ochieng is a highly experienced Consultant Obstetrician and Gynaecologist with more than 25 years of dedicated service in women's reproductive health.

He currently practices at Ajuoga Hospital in Nairobi, where he specializes in high-risk obstetrics, minimally invasive gynaecological surgery, fertility management, and comprehensive antenatal & postnatal care.

Qualifications: MBChB (JKUAT), MMed Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Registration: Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentists Council (KMPDC)
